## v0.1.0-mvp

ManagerMeet turns rough app ideas into build-ready Android product plans for
managers. The MVP includes Dashboard, Add/Edit, Detail, Settings, and Export
screens; Room-backed project storage; DataStore preferences; demo data;
light/dark mode; validation; edit/delete/archive support; and local
Codex-ready prompt generation.

Privacy stance: no login, no cloud, no paid APIs, and no uploads in v1.
Your app ideas stay on this device.
